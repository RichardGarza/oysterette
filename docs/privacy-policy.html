Privacy Policy for Oysterette

Last Updated: November 7, 2025 Effective Date: November 7, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Oysterette ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for the Oysterette mobile application (the "App").

By using Oysterette, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide Directly

Account Information:

  • Email address (required for registration)
  • Display name (required for registration)
  • Password (encrypted and hashed)

    • User-Generated Content:

  • Oyster reviews (ratings, tasting notes, attribute scores)
  • Favorite oysters list
  • Review votes (agree/disagree on other users' reviews)
  • Oyster submissions (community database contributions)

    • 2. Information Collected Automatically

    Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Device identifiers (for crash reporting only)

    • Usage Information:

  • Features used within the app
  • Time spent in the app
  • Navigation patterns
  • Crash logs and error reports

    • 3. Information from Third-Party Services

    Google Sign-In:

  • If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive:
  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile picture URL (not currently used)
  • Google account ID (for authentication only)

    • We do NOT receive your Google password or access to other Google services.

    How We Use Your Information

    We use the collected information for the following purposes:

    1. Service Provision

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate your identity
  • Display your reviews and ratings
  • Sync favorites across devices
  • Calculate credibility scores
  • Generate personalized recommendations

    • 2. App Improvement

  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Improve user experience
  • Develop new features
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Optimize performance

    • 3. Communication

  • Send important service updates
  • Respond to support requests
  • Notify you of changes to policies (if required)

    • We do NOT:

  • Send marketing emails
  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your information for advertising purposes

    • Data Storage and Security

    Storage Locations

    Local Storage (On Your Device):

  • Authentication token (encrypted)
  • User preferences (theme, settings)
  • Favorite oysters list (synced with server)
  • Cached app data

    • Server Storage (Secure Cloud Database):

  • Account information
  • Reviews and ratings
  • Favorites
  • User statistics
  • Hosted on Neon (PostgreSQL) with encryption at rest

    • Backend Server:

  • Hosted on Railway with HTTPS encryption
  • Located in United States
  • SOC 2 compliant infrastructure

    • Security Measures

    We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Password Security: Passwords hashed using bcrypt (industry standard)
  • Token-Based Authentication: JWT tokens with expiration
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against brute-force attacks
  • Input Validation: Prevention of SQL injection and XSS attacks
  • Secure Storage: AsyncStorage for local data (encrypted by OS)

    • Data Retention

    • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
    • Deleted Accounts: All personal data deleted within 30 days
    • Reviews: May be anonymized instead of deleted to preserve community ratings
    • Backups: Retained for 30 days for disaster recovery

    Your Rights and Choices

    Access and Update

  • View your profile and reviews in the app
  • Update your name and email in Settings
  • Change your password at any time

    • Privacy Settings

  • Control profile visibility (Public/Friends/Private)
  • Choose what appears on your profile
  • Hide review history, favorites, or statistics

    • Data Deletion

    You have the right to request deletion of your data: 1. In-App: Settings → Delete Account 2. Email Request: Send request to support@oysterette.app

    Upon deletion request, we will:

  • Immediately deactivate your account
  • Delete all personal information within 30 days
  • Optionally anonymize reviews to preserve community data

    • Data Export

    Request a copy of your data by emailing support@oysterette.app. We will provide:
  • Account information
  • All reviews and ratings
  • Favorites list
  • User statistics

    • Format: JSON or CSV (your choice) Delivery: Within 30 days of request

    Third-Party Services

    Google Sign-In

  • Provider: Google LLC
  • Data Shared: Email, name, profile ID
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Purpose: Authentication only

    • Hosting Services

  • Railway (Backend): https://railway.app/legal/privacy
  • Neon (Database): https://neon.tech/privacy-policy

    • We have data processing agreements with all service providers.

    Children's Privacy

    Oysterette is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

    Age Restriction: 4+ (App Stores rating) Content: Food and beverage reviews only, no objectionable content

    International Users

    Oysterette is operated from the United States. If you are accessing the app from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States.

    European Economic Area (EEA) Users: Under GDPR, you have additional rights including:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

    • To exercise these rights, contact support@oysterette.app.

    Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app and on our website. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • In-app notification
  • Email (if you've provided consent)
  • App update notes

    • Continued use of Oysterette after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

    Contact Us

    If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy:

    Email: support@oysterette.app Website: https://richardgarza.github.io/oysterette/docs/ Address: c/o Richard Garza (business address to be updated)

    Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours for privacy-related inquiries.

    Consent

    By using Oysterette, you consent to:

  • Collection of information as described
  • Processing of your data for stated purposes
  • Transfer of data to service providers
  • Use of cookies and local storage

    • You may withdraw consent by deleting your account.

    Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

    We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual Necessity: To provide the service you've requested
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our service and prevent fraud
  • Consent: For optional features like recommendations
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws

    • Data Breach Notification

    In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Provide details of the breach
  • Explain steps taken to address it
  • Offer guidance on protective measures

    • Additional Disclosures

    California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

    California residents have additional rights:
  • Right to know what data we collect
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of data "sales" (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising rights

    • Do Not Track

    We do not track users across third-party websites. The app does not respond to Do Not Track signals as it doesn't track for advertising.
